About

I am an M.E. student in Computational Science and Engineering at Harvard University. My research interests include robust representation learning, multimodal learning, and geometric machine learning, with applications to embodied and scientific AI. Previously, I studied mathematics and computer science at the University of Michigan and conducted research spanning machine learning, algebraic combinatorics, and computational biology.
